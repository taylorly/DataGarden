A Writer's Room

This data visualization is documentation of the beginning of my writing journey. Over the course of fourteen days, I recorded my writing habits in my journal. The goal was to measure how much I write and where I wrote more words while writing in the confines of my room.

The flower represents another way to visualize the amount of words I wrote. Since this fourteen day experience, I have began to write my novel. I am handwriting my first draft and I have what I presume is thousands of words. At first glance, the trend line may not seem like much. However, I know how much I have grown since then. I understand now how important it is to climb over the hill of intimidation and ignore the allure of perfection that prevents some of the truest writing coming into fruition.

Every Word Counts.
